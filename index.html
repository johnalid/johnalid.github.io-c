Aims and Objectives

Spiritual Growth

Enhansing the spiritual development of members, through personal prayers, spiritual reading of the Bible and participation in the sacramental and missionary life of the church .

Catholic Identity

It promotes Catholic identity through inculcation of Catholic doctrines and practices as containd in the traditions and teachings of the Catholic Church. With Cyon, The church catches the youth young into the faith.

Promotes Good Christian Life

It seek to promotes the virtue of a good Christian life by encouraging and praying for vocations to the priesthood and religious life among its members .

Networking among Youths

Cyon serves as a forum where youths meet to interact and further network among themselves thereby allowing for exchange of ideas, philosophies and knowledge.
